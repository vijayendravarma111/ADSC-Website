PRAESTO 2026

About Praesto

Praesto is the flagship annual technical event of the Data Science branch at our college, a five–day celebration of innovation, hands‑on learning, and the emerging technologies shaping the future of the software industry.

The 2026 edition runs from March 9–13 as an intensive experiential learning program, featuring 4–5 cutting‑edge workshops, live project building, and mentor‑driven sessions designed to upskill students for the next wave of tech opportunities.

Key Focus Domains

Generative AI

Prompt engineering, LLM workflows, and creative AI applications for real‑world use‑cases.

Data Analytics

Data pipelines, dashboards, and insight‑driven decision making using modern analytics tools.

DevOps

CI/CD, containerization, and cloud‑native practices for reliable software delivery.

Blockchain Development

Smart contracts, decentralized applications, and secure transaction design.

UI/UX & Frontend

Design systems, accessible interfaces, and modern frontend engineering workflows.

Ethical Hacking

Security fundamentals, attack simulations, and responsible vulnerability assessment.

Trainers & Jury

Throughout Praesto 2026, students are mentored by trainers and domain experts with strong academic foundations and practical industry exposure in AI, Data Science, Full‑Stack Development, Cloud, and GenAI.

The Project Development Challenge is evaluated by a distinguished jury panel of senior faculty and external professionals, ensuring transparent and unbiased evaluation.

Projects are assessed on innovation, technical depth, real‑world applicability, scalability, design quality, and clarity of presentation.
